Case Study Overview

In today’s regulatory landscape, data privacy and security are critical, especially for organizations handling sensitive information. For TransferMate, managing and safeguarding large volumes of data posed significant challenges, particularly when it came to deleting outdated or unnecessary data while ensuring compliance with industry regulations such as GDPR.

With this project we aimed to review and delete any personal data which fell under the general data protection guidelines so as to be complaint to GDPR standards.
