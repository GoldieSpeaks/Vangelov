This project involved designing and delivering a comprehensive payment platform for INSEAD, a leading international business school. The platform enabled students, sponsors, and alumni to facilitate tuition payments and program fees through a fully branded, secure web solution with support for payment plans, installments, and multi-currency transactions.

As Product Owner, I led the end-to-end delivery of this complex integration project, coordinating cross-functional teams across discovery, design, development, and stakeholder management to build a scalable platform that integrated with INSEAD's existing systems and our internal payment infrastructure.

The Challenge
Building a Custom Payment Platform from the Ground Up
INSEAD needed a fully integrated payment solution that could handle complex payment scenarios while maintaining effective integration with their existing enterprise systems.

The challenge was to build a comprehensive payment platform that would serve as the primary payment interface for thousands of students and sponsors worldwide. The platform needed to support flexible payment plans, installment options, and multi-currency transactions while integrating with INSEAD's existing infrastructure including PeopleSoft (their enterprise system) and ADFS (their authentication system).

As Product Owner, I was responsible for leading the entire project lifecycle‚Äîfrom initial discovery and requirements gathering through design, development, and production deployment. This required coordinating multiple teams, managing complex stakeholder relationships, and ensuring effective integration across disparate systems.

Complex System Integration

The platform required integration with multiple enterprise systems: INSEAD's PeopleSoft ERP system, ADFS authentication infrastructure, and our internal payment CRM and backoffice systems. Each integration had unique technical requirements, data formats, and security protocols that needed to be carefully mapped and implemented.

PeopleSoft Coordination

INSEAD's PeopleSoft team needed to develop additional features and API endpoints on their platform to accommodate the integration requirements. This required extensive coordination, technical documentation, and alignment on data schemas and workflow triggers between our teams and theirs.

Multi-Currency Payment Logic

The platform needed to support students and sponsors from around the world making payments in different base currencies. This required custom payment logic to handle currency conversion, exchange rate management, settlement in multiple currencies, and proper accounting reconciliation across different financial systems.

ADFS Authentication & 2FA

Students needed to authenticate using their INSEAD credentials through ADFS (Active Directory Federation Services). This required implementing a secure single sign-on (SSO) flow that integrated our platform with INSEAD's identity management system while maintaining robust security and user experience standards.

Custom Branding & UX

The platform needed to be fully branded to match INSEAD's visual identity and user experience standards. This involved close collaboration with their design requirements, creating a custom UI that maintained brand consistency while delivering intuitive payment workflows for both students and sponsors.

Payment Plans & Installments

The platform needed sophisticated logic to handle flexible payment plans and installment schedules. This included automated payment scheduling, missed payment handling, partial payment support, and real-time synchronization with INSEAD's student accounts system through the PeopleSoft integration.

My Role & Approach
Leading the Project End-to-End
As Product Owner, I led both internal teams and INSEAD's technical and business teams through the entire project lifecycle, from initial discovery through production deployment.
Discovery & Requirements Gathering

I led comprehensive discovery workshops with INSEAD stakeholders across finance, IT, student services, and admissions to understand their requirements, existing systems, and business processes. This involved mapping current payment workflows, identifying pain points, and defining success criteria for the new platform.

Cross-Organizational Leadership

I led coordination efforts between our internal teams and INSEAD's technical teams, including their PeopleSoft developers and IT infrastructure groups. This required facilitating joint planning sessions, making decisions that affected both organizations, and sometimes directly leading INSEAD's teams to maintain project momentum and alignment.

Business Presentations & Stakeholder Engagement

I delivered regular presentations to INSEAD's business stakeholders, engaging in a continuous refinement process. This involved presenting designs, prototypes, and solutions, gathering feedback, iterating based on their input, and maintaining ongoing dialogue until we achieved alignment on requirements and approach.

Product Roadmap & Planning

I created detailed product roadmaps that broke down the complex project into manageable phases and iterative development cycles. This included prioritizing features, sequencing integration work, and establishing clear milestones that aligned with INSEAD's academic calendar and business needs.

Design Team Leadership

I worked closely with UI/UX designers to create a payment experience that balanced INSEAD's brand requirements with usability best practices. This involved reviewing mockups, validating user flows, and ensuring the design accommodated all required payment scenarios including plans, installments, and multi-currency transactions.

Technical Architecture & Integration

I collaborated with our Business Analyst on system logic mapping and API design. Together we defined the integration architecture between our payment platform, INSEAD's PeopleSoft system, and ADFS authentication. This included data flow diagrams, API specifications, and error handling protocols.

PeopleSoft API Integration Layer

Built a comprehensive API integration layer connecting our payment platform with INSEAD's PeopleSoft ERP system. This involved coordinating with the PeopleSoft team to develop additional endpoints, defining data schemas, implementing real-time synchronization of student accounts, and establishing robust error handling and retry mechanisms.

Payment Plans & Installment Management Across Multiple Systems

Created sophisticated payment plan logic allowing students to split tuition payments into customizable installments across multiple integrated systems. The system automatically schedules payments, sends reminders, processes recurring charges, and syncs payment status back to PeopleSoft in real-time.

Multi-Currency Payment Processing

Implemented custom payment logic to handle transactions in multiple base currencies, supporting INSEAD's global student population. This included currency conversion, dynamic exchange rate management, and proper settlement reconciliation across different financial systems.

Payment Matrices

Developed comprehensive payment matrices to handle complex pricing structures, discounts, and fee calculations for different student categories, programs, and sponsorship arrangements. This ensured accurate billing and transparent cost structures for all payment scenarios.

INSEAD Payment Operations Dashboard

Built a custom administrative dashboard for INSEAD's payments and finance teams to monitor payments, manage payment plans, process refunds, and generate reports. The dashboard provided real-time visibility into payment activity and integrated with their existing financial workflows.

Branded UI/UX & Custom Email System

Created a fully branded user experience that matched INSEAD's visual identity and institutional standards. Developed custom email templates for payment confirmations, reminders, and notifications that maintained brand consistency while providing clear, actionable information to students and sponsors.

Payment Reminder & Notification System

Implemented automated email notification system for payment reminders, confirmations, and status updates tailored for students and sponsors. The system sends personalized communications based on payment schedules, deadlines, and account status to ensure timely payments and clear communication.

SMS Payment Reminders

Added SMS-based payment reminders as an additional communication channel to reach students and sponsors globally. The SMS system provides critical payment deadline notifications and status updates, complementing the email system for maximum reach and effectiveness.

Key Learnings
Insights from the Journey
Cross-Organizational Leadership is Critical

Leading teams across organizational boundaries required a different skill set than managing internal teams. Building trust with INSEAD's technical and business teams, respecting their processes while driving toward project goals, and knowing when to lead versus when to collaborate was essential to project success.

Continuous Refinement Beats Perfect Planning

Rather than trying to define every requirement upfront, we embraced a continuous refinement process. Regular presentations to INSEAD's business stakeholders, gathering feedback, iterating on designs and solutions, and adjusting course based on learnings created better outcomes than attempting to create a "perfect" plan at the start.

Discovery Upfront Saves Time Later

Investing significant time in discovery and requirements gathering at the project's start paid enormous dividends during development. Understanding INSEAD's business processes, system architecture, and user needs in depth allowed us to build the right solution and avoid costly rework.

Technical Architecture is Product Strategy

As Product Owner, understanding the technical architecture and integration points was critical to making informed product decisions. Working closely with the Business Analyst on API design and system mapping enabled better prioritization and more realistic roadmap planning.
