0.2.1 To Do List - Android

To Do List stands as a formidable competitor in the market, boasting an impressive record of over 10 million downloads and an outstanding user rating of 4.9. It was the initial app we extensively researched, leaving us astonished by its remarkable performance.

The existing Design Language of the application leaves much to be desired, resembling a product created using generic app-editing tools found online. While the user experience is commendably straightforward, we believe there is ample room for improvement. Upon initial assessment, it became apparent that with minimal effort, we could significantly enhance the Design Language and User Experience, surpassing the current standards.

0.2.2 Tasks.org - Android

Tasks.org emerges as the second task manager under review, garnering a relatively lower download count of 100,000 but maintaining a comparable user rating.

Much like its counterpart, To Do List, Tasks.org exhibits a similar homepage layout; however, there are discernible improvements in its design language. Nonetheless, I find some aspects of the side menu unsatisfactory—both in terms of visual appeal and usability. The overwhelming appearance and perceived complexity detract from the application's purpose as a simple local task manager. Consequently, my determination to create a superior visual representation only intensified after evaluating Tasks.org.

0.2.2 Tasks - Android

Tasks became the third application I chose to download, boasting a considerable number of downloads surpassing 100,000, which initially gave it a semblance of being a decent application.

However, after a mere 5 minutes of using it, I found myself questioning the very purpose of its existence. Every aspect of the application was abysmal, leaving me with no inclination to elaborate further on its shortcomings.


Research Outcome

After conducting a thorough day-long research and evaluation of the three apps in question, we discovered a common thread: their simplicity and user-friendliness. While these apps effectively fulfilled their intended purpose, we observed that their visual presentation fell short of expectations, with the exception of the third app, which proved to be unsatisfactory both in terms of aesthetics and functionality.

This valuable research led us to a significant realization: we prioritize a streamlined and responsive end product over an abundance of features. We found that the utilization of overly complex task managers often overwhelmed users, particularly when dealing with simple tasks like note-taking. As we move forward, we advocate for a cleaner and more efficient approach, where functionality seamlessly integrates with an uncluttered design. Our focus lies in delivering solutions that strike a balance between user-friendly interfaces and effective functionality, ensuring an optimal user experience for our clients.

0.2 Wireframes and Ideation

What best suits the users needs?
After going through user research, we decided to move ahead with wireframing and prototyping.

During the wireframing process for Brave Notes, we strategically planned and visualized the app's layout and functionality. By creating low-fidelity wireframes, we established the core structure and element placement, facilitating efficient iteration and valuable feedback. The wireframes acted as a guide for development, resulting in a user-centric design and a seamless experience for Brave Notes users.

Turning the wireframes into art

0.3.1 Design Language

Clean and Simple

The active tasks and notes pages aren't really much different from the reviewed apps. We reimagined the design, implementing a clean typographical hierarchy that immediately grabs the user's attention. This approach, inspired by successful implementations by Apple, has proven to be highly effective and well-received by users, including ourselves. By incorporating similar design principles, we strive to deliver a seamless user experience that aligns with user preferences.

The end result is a great approach which brings a new approach and user experience which is currently not present on the martketpalce.

0.3.2 Ui Design

Stylish Simplicity

Our app's UI reflects a meticulous approach that balances aesthetics and usability. We carefully chose colors, typography, and icons to align with the app's brand identity. Emphasizing intuitive navigation and user-centered design, we created an interface that is visually captivating and easy to use. Incorporating user feedback throughout the design process, we ensured a delightful user experience.

0.3.2 Colors of Choice

Chosing the Right Blend

The general colours for the project consist of carefully selected colours, designed to stand out across the competitive set.


They aim to provide a soft feel that doesn’t create excess focus points that obstruct attention.

0.3.3 Typography

Yantramanav

We selected Yantramanav as our typography font for its perfect blend of modernity and readability. With its clean lines and balanced proportions, Yantramanav adds a touch of sophistication while ensuring optimal legibility. Its versatility allows for seamless integration into our app's design, elevating the overall visual experience.
