Project Overview


Description

One of the first things that stood out for me when I first got onboarded at TransferMate, specifically for the education project was the lack of a knowledge base. This made my onboarding a really rough one and proved to make navigation within the project a living nightmare.

As part of my internal yellow belt training for Lean Six Sigma, I had to complete and deliver a successful Six Sigma project which I decided would be the creation of a knowledge base within a confluence space.

Role and Responsibilities:

My main roles was to map out the knowledge base layout, user flow, hierarchy and structure. After that, to execute the ideation into a tangiable expression in Confluence. Additionally to that I also ahd to train the teams to use and navigate the new system.
