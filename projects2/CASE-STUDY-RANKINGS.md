## Tier Summary

**Tier 1 — Flagship (1-5):** Multi-month, multi-system, high strategic value. These alone prove senior PO capability.

**Tier 2 — Strong (6-14):** Significant scope with clear business outcomes. Good standalone showcase pieces.

**Tier 3 — Solid (15-26):** Well-executed deliveries with real complexity. Support breadth of experience.

**Tier 4 — Targeted (27-33):** Focused integrations/optimisations. Show hands-on delivery range.

**Tier 5 — Functional (34-39):** Quick wins. Prove you can deliver small-scope cleanly too.
